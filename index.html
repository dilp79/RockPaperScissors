How to Play

Place your piece on any empty hex. Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.

✊✌️🖐️

The Board

The game is played on a hexagonal grid. Each piece can interact with its neighbors.

⬡

Battles

When your piece beats an adjacent enemy piece and is not threatened, the enemy is cleared. Score points for each capture!
